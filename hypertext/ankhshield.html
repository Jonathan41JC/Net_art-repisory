Ankh Shield



To Craft the Ankh Shield, you will need the following materials:
  • Ankh Charm
  • Medicated Bandage
    • Crafting

  • Countercourse Mantra
    • Crafting

  • Armor Bracing
    • Crafting

  • Reflective Shades
    • Crafting

  • The Plan
    • Crafting

  • Obsidian Shield
    • Crafting

Combine the obsidian shield and the ankh charm in the thinkers workshop

Ankh Shield Buffs/Immunities of this object

When you equip the Ankh Shield, you will be immune to the following debuffs:
  • Bleeding

  • Broken Armor

  • Burning

  • Confused

  • Cursed

  • Darkness

  • Poisoned

  • Silenced

  • Slow

  • Weak

  • Chilled

  • Stoned
