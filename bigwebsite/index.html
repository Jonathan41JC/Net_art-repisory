IT (2017) – Resume

The first film follows a group of kids in Derry who are terrorized by Pennywise, a shape-shifting entity that feeds on fear. As children begin disappearing, the Losers' Club comes together to confront the evil hiding in the sewers.

IT (2017) – Review

This movie blends horror and friendship perfectly. The performances feel real,the scares are intense, and Pennywise stands out as one of the most memorable horror villains of modern cinema.

IT Chapter Two (2019) – Resume

The Losers return to Derry as adults after Pennywise resurfaces. They must face both their childhood trauma and the terrifying clown once again to finally destroy the evil haunting their town.

IT Chapter Two (2019) – Review

The sequel expands the emotional depth of the story. It explores memory, fear, and growth while delivering disturbing visuals and strong performances from the adult cast.

Welcome to Derry (Series) – Resume

The prequel series explores the origins of Pennywise and the dark history of Derry. It dives deeper into how fear has shaped the town across generations.

Welcome to Derry – Review

The series expands the universe in a compelling way, giving fans more lore, suspense, and psychological horror while maintaining the disturbing tone of the films.
