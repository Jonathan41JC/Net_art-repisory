Arkeanos r/place Art Piece

Arkeanos Community & Nexxuz

Year:2022 to 2023

This piece comes from r/place and represents a logo made by the Arkeanos community, which is a group formed around a well known YouTuber that built a strong online following. I was part of that community and helped work on this logo during the event. Everyone could only place one pixel at a time, usually every few minutes, and at the beginning it was even slower, so it took a lot of coordination to actually build anything. At the same time, other groups would try to take over or destroy parts of the design, so we had to constantly fix and defend it. It felt less like just making art and more like being part of a shared online effort where collaboration and conflict were happening at the same time.
