The Million Dollar Homepage

Alex Tew

Year: 2005

This project is a website made up of one million pixels, where each pixel was sold as ad space. People and companies could buy small sections of the page to promote themselves, so the whole thing turns into a crowded grid of images and links. What is interesting is how it feels like a kind of economic competition, where everyone is trying to get attention in a limited space. Instead of fighting with pixels like in r/place, here people are competing through money, but it still creates this sense of pressure and control over digital space.
